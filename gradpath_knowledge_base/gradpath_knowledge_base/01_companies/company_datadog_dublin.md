## Role Profile · Graduate / Junior Level
- Typical role titles seen:
  - Customer Success Associate
  - Front Desk Associate
- Required qualifications:
  - Customer Success Associate: 0-2 years of experience in a customer account facing role; strong communication skills; desire to grow a career in Customer Success.
  - Front Desk Associate: 1-3 years administrative/office experience; excellent verbal and written communication skills; high-quality customer service orientation.
- Required technical skills:
  - Customer Success Associate: Ability to negotiate contract terms, manage customer relationships, and handle account management tasks.
  - Front Desk Associate: Administrative skills, office management, vendor coordination, and event planning.
- Nice-to-have skills:
  - Customer-minded approach, adaptability, attention to detail, problem-solving skills, willingness to work occasional overtime (Front Desk Associate).
  - Experience with up-sell/cross-sell activities (Customer Success Associate).
- Stated working model:
  - Hybrid workplace: "We operate as a hybrid workplace to ensure our Datadogs can create a work-life harmony that best fits them."
